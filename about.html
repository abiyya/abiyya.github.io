A little bit of me

Born 21 years ago in Cleveland, Ohio, I currently attend Cleveland Institute of Art as a first year student. As I pursue a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts, my intended major is Drawing. My art is a combination of many different mediums. I am most versed in analogue art forms and sketching with digital painting coming in second. I've recently began developing my 3D designing skills both digitally and physically. The works that I have provided here are a reflection of my digital art content.
